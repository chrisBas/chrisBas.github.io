Pages

Description:

Small JS/CSS library that enables one to navigate through 'pages' on a single webpage.

Code:
<div class="pages">
  <div class="page">
    Page-1 content goes here...
  </div>
  <div class="page">
    Page-2 content goes here...
  </div>
</div>
